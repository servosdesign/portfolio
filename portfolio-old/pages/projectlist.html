Servos Marketplace

An Ethereum based NFT marketplace application (dApp) made with React, Solidity, Truffle, and web3.js which allows the user to view and / or purchase ERC721 NFTs from among the marketplace items. User log in with their ether wallet address and have access to purchase Solidty / Truffle generated ERC721 NFTs made on the Rinkeby's development network. Featuring custom artwork made by yours truly.

Solidity, Truffle, web3.js, React

Crypto Watchlist

Crypto Watchlist is a Cryptocurrency App, powered by RapidAPI, Chart.js, and Redux, which shows top cryptocurrencies, news updates, and exchange data. This app takes use of thing like fetching data from an api, changing chart data, and updated news feeds for highlighted values. Users can search from top 100 coins and filter through results to find their choice.

React, Redux, Chart.js, RapidAPI
